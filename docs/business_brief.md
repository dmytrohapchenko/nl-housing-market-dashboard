# Business Brief

## Project Purpose

This project builds a reporting-ready SQL layer for analyzing the Dutch housing market by region and quarter. The goal is to support a Power BI dashboard that shows price trends, sales activity, and regional comparisons.

## Business Context

Housing market reporting often needs clear period-over-period comparisons. This project focuses on quarterly changes in the house price index, sold dwellings, and purchase values across Dutch COROP regions.

## Target Users

- Business analysts reviewing regional housing market performance
- Dashboard users comparing latest-quarter regional results
- Portfolio reviewers assessing SQL modeling and reporting skills

## Key Business Questions

- How did the house price index change over time?
- Which regions had the strongest YoY house price growth in the latest quarter?
- Which regions had the weakest QoQ house price change in the latest quarter?
- Which regions had the highest number of sold dwellings?
- Which regions are above or below the average house price index?
- What share of national sold dwellings does each region represent?

## Expected Reporting Output

The expected output is a Power BI dashboard based on a validated SQL dimensional model. Planned visuals include KPI cards, quarterly trend charts, regional ranking tables, regional comparisons, and a latest-quarter reporting snapshot.
